Contact Project BISmark

Do you need help? Visit the help page first.

General Inquiries

Please take a look at our frequently asked questions first to find answers to common questions. If you're looking for a specific member of the BISmark team, please check the contributors page.

If you still have a question or otherwise need to contact us, please mail bismark-core@projectbismark.net.

Feedback

We want to make BISmark better for home router users, researchers, and the open source software community. If you have found a bug or want to offer suggestions for how to make BISmark better, please let us know:

  • Send mail to the BISmark developers mailing list
  • Discuss your issues or ideas on our bismark-discuss mailing list

You can track the progress of development, including specific features or bug fixes, using the project management tools found at our open development nexus.

Security

If you have discovered a security vulnerability with the BISmark platform or web applications, or need to report a security-related event, please contact us at bismark-core@projectbismark.net.

If you deem it necessary, you may use PGP key F18688B8 to encrypt your communications to a member of our team.
