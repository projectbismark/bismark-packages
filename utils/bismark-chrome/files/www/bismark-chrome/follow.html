Follow Project BISmark

If you are interested in keeping up-to-date with Project BISmark and the research we are doing, we have a number of ways to stay apprised of our work and activities.

Project blog

Follow our project blog for relatively informal, somewhat timely updates on our activities.

Publications and research

We intend to publish a number of academic papers and other research outputs based on the data and observations collected through this project. Please visit projectbismark.net for the latest list of publications and other work.

Development activity

If you're interested in the software and development aspects of BISmark, you can watch our project management activity and follow our Github repositories at the open development nexus.
