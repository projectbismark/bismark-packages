Welcome to your BISmark home router

Welcome, and thank you for participating in Project BISmark.

BISmark is a project to measure the performance of residential broadband Internet service from the home. BISmark will soon also study and measure networks inside the home, and the way people use them.

This home router (also known as a gateway) is the key to your participation in the BISmark project. The router contains software to measure your Internet connection and report the results. You can use the BISmark router like any other wireless home router.

Project BISmark needs your help to collect high-quality data. Please take care of your BISmark router as described in the participation agreement. Most importantly:

  • Keep the BISmark router connected to the Internet and powered on at all times.
  • Let us know if you move or change Internet providers or service plans.
  • Use your Internet service as you normally would.
